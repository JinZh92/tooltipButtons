Implemented features:
  • Basic tooltip requirements.
  • Top/bottom edge detection and change tooltip location accordingly.
  • Press 'Esc' key to close all tooltips
  • Clicking outside of tooltip will close it; Clicking inside it will not.
  • Responsive design (Shrinking resolution to smaller then 960px to have buttons displayed vertically.)
